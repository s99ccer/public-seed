# 7. Jacobo Grinberg-Zylberbaum

## Who Was He?

**Jacobo Grinberg-Zylberbaum** (1946–1994) was a Mexican neurophysiologist who combined rigorous scientific methodology with shamanic traditions, deep meditation, and indigenous healing practices. He founded the National Institute for the Study of Consciousness, authored **50+ books**, and developed **Syntergic Theory (Teoría Sintérgica)** — a unified field theory of consciousness.

On **December 8, 1994**, days before his 48th birthday, Grinberg walked out of his home and vanished without a trace. His laboratory was found completely empty — all research data, equipment, notes, and experimental records gone. His disappearance remains unsolved.

## Core Books (Fully Read)

### 1. La Teoría Sintérgica (The Syntergic Theory) — 86p, 139K chars

**Main Thesis:** Consciousness and physical reality are two poles of a unified informational continuum called **Syntergy (Sintergia)**. Experience is not generated *by* the brain alone but emerges from the **interaction** between two fields.

**The Core Equation:**

> **Experience (경험) = Neuronal Field (신경장) × Quantum Field (양자장)**

**Key Concepts:**
- **Syntergy:** Degree of internal coherence, organization, and informational integration of a system. High syntergy → high inclusion (each part contains information about the whole) → emergence of consciousness.
- **Neuronal Field:** Electromagnetic/energetic field generated by neural activity that extends beyond the skull into surrounding space. Its morphology encodes brain state, memory, and perception.
- **Quantum Field:** The fundamental energetic organization of space-time — not empty but a structured informational matrix (the Lattice).
- **Experience = Neuronal Field × Quantum Field:** Perception occurs when these two fields interact, creating an interference pattern that is decoded as qualia.
- **Syntergic Communication:** Under high syntergy states (e.g., deep meditation), neuronal fields from different brains can interact directly, enabling telepathy and non-local effects.
- **Inclusion Principle:** Complex systems organize hierarchically — each level contains information about higher and lower levels (holographic logic).
- **Gravitational Modification:** High-syntergy brains can alter local gravitational fields (levitation, weight change).

**Experiments/Evidence:**
- EEG coherence studies correlating high inter/intra-hemispheric coherence with syntergic states
- Communication experiments where subjects in Faraday cages demonstrated information transfer correlated with EEG syntergy
- Weight reduction experiments on objects in shielded chambers

**Author's Conclusions:**
- Consciousness is not reducible to neural activity; it is a field phenomenon
- Reality is a co-creation between the observer and the observed
- The universe is a unified informational continuum where mind and matter converge

### 2. El Cerebro Consciente (The Conscious Brain) — 84p, 289K chars

**Main Thesis:** Consciousness is the result of the brain operating in a state of high **neurosyntergy** — when neural activity achieves sufficient coherence, inclusiveness, and integration, consciousness emerges as a self-referential property of the system.

**Key Concepts:**
- **Neurosyntergy:** Neural systems function along a continuum from low syntergy (fragmented, local processing) to high syntergy (global coherence, high inclusion). Consciousness appears above a threshold.
- **Self-reference:** The brain's ability to algorithmically include itself in its own processing → self-awareness
- **Convergence-Divergence Theory:** Neural information flows converge into high-order inclusive algorithms (abstract concepts, self) then diverge back into specific responses
- **Central Processor:** Higher-order neural algorithms that integrate all sensory and cognitive information into a unified experiential field
- **Present Expansion (Expansión del Presente):** Under high syntergy, the subjective "present moment" expands, allowing access to normally unconscious micro-processes
- **Memory:** Not stored locally but reconstructed through convergence-divergence dynamics
- **Experience-Consciousness Identity:** When conscious awareness and experience become identical → access to the **Ser (Being)**

**Experiments/Evidence:**
- EEG studies showing coherence patterns correlate with conscious states
- Evoked potentials and concept formation experiments
- Split-brain and hemisphere studies reinterpreted through syntergic lens

**Author's Conclusions:**
- The brain is a "syntergic organ" — its purpose is to create increasingly inclusive, coherent states
- Consciousness is an emergent property of high neural organization, but extends beyond the brain through the neuronal field
- Self-awareness = the brain's algorithm containing itself
- Healing and expanded perception are attainable through training syntergy (meditation, self-inquiry)

### 3. Los Fundamentos de la Experiencia (The Foundations of Experience) — 87p, 327K chars

**Main Thesis:** Experience is a **creation** — not a passive reception of external stimuli. The book systematically deconstructs the naive realist assumption and builds a psychophysiological model of how the brain constructs experiential reality.

**Key Concepts:**
- **Experience as Creation:** Every percept, emotion, and thought is actively constructed by the brain's interaction with the quantum field. "Exterior" reality does not exist independently — it is co-created.
- **Algorithmic Model of Perception:** The brain operates through hierarchical algorithms — sensory input is raw "energy soup" that gets increasingly structured into objects, concepts, meanings through convergence.
- **The Quantum Brain:** Neuronal activity and quantum field interactions occur at multiple scales; brain function is not purely classical but involves quantum-level coherence.
- **Perceptual Transparency:** We see "through" space because we cannot simultaneously decode all quantum points — space appears empty but is actually a solid energetic continuum.
- **Self as Algorithmic Convergence:** The sense of self (Yo) is the highest convergence point of neural algorithms — the most inclusive pattern.
- **Ontogenetic Syntergization:** As children develop, their brains become more syntergic, more inclusive, more capable of abstraction and self-reference.
- **Pathology as Low Syntergy:** Mental illness, dissociation, and fragmentation are states of low neural coherence.

**Experiments/Evidence:**
- Detailed psychophysiological experiments on perception, memory, and concept formation
- Studies on sensory deprivation, meditation, and altered states
- Reinterpretation of classical perception experiments through the syntergic lens

**Author's Conclusions:**
- Reality is not given — it is a continuous creation
- Knowledge of this creative process is liberating: one can learn to "create reality" consciously
- The "Foundations of Experience" are the interaction between neuronal field and quantum field
- The path to liberation is recognizing oneself as the creator of one's own experience

### 4. La Luz Angelmática (The Angelmatic Light) — 180p, 406K chars

**Main Thesis:** This is Grinberg's most personal and mystical book — a blend of **autobiographical narrative** and **experimental report** on extraocular vision. It describes his own spiritual journeys and philosophical reflections on consciousness, being, time, and the "angelmatic light" — the light of consciousness witnessing itself.

**Key Concepts (Philosophical):**
- **Luz Angelmática (Angelmátic Light):** The experience of becoming aware that one is aware — meta-awareness, the light of consciousness itself. It is the connection between individual consciousness and the "above" (true identity).
- **Ser (Being):** Pure existence without content. Independent, non-local, absolute freedom, pure love.
- **Expansion of Time / Lentificación:** Accessing normally unconscious processes by slowing subjective time; this is the secret to wisdom, accessing the unconscious, and seeing past/future lives.
- **Consciousness of Consciousness:** Metacognition — a qualitative leap beyond ordinary awareness; witnessing the witness.
- **Experience-Consciousness Identity:** The mature state where experience and awareness become synonymous.
- **Ego Expansion:** The true way to dissolve the ego is not by suppressing it but by expanding it until it encompasses everything — then the ego disappears because all is One.
- **Intuition:** Direct contact with information contained in the energetic organization of space; develops through validation like any learned skill.
- **Pattern Formation:** Actions/thoughts weave a pattern; when the pattern becomes visible, a leap occurs to a higher level of awareness.

**Extraocular Vision Experiment (Appendix I):**
- 19 children (ages 5–13) trained to see without using their eyes
- Training protocol: breathing exercises → meditation on "Who am I?" → energy visualization → blindfolded viewing
- **Results:** Children could read, describe colors, perceive internal organs, diagnose illness, and even heal. Vision was omnidirectional (could see behind themselves).
- **Quartz Crystal Effect:** Holding a quartz crystal dramatically enhanced extraocular vision, adding tridimensionality and clarity. Theoretical explanation: quartz's molecular structure (tetrahedral spirals at X-ray wavelengths) may increase the syntergy of the neuronal field, facilitating quantum field interaction.
- **Age Limit:** The phenomenon was difficult to evoke after ~15 years of age.
- **Quantum/Discrete Appearance:** Extraocular vision appeared all-at-once (quantum leap) rather than gradually — suggesting a threshold phenomenon.

**Author's Conclusions:**
- The "angelmatic light" is the ultimate goal: permanent ecstasy through recognition of oneself as creator of experience
- Extraocular vision confirms the Syntergic Theory: it is the same process as normal vision but without retinal transduction, proving that perception is field interaction, not sensory processing
- The children with the strongest sense of self-identity learned fastest — confirming that self-awareness facilitates direct decoding of the quantum field
- Body-based intelligence ("sabiduría corporal") can be accessed and developed; illness can be a "solution" the body finds for unconscious conflicts
- The true identity is not the body or mind but the witness that transcends them

## The Lattice — What Grinberg Actually Discovered

Grinberg's central discovery can be summarized in three statements:

1. **Space is not empty.** It is an organized energetic continuum (the Lattice/Quantum Field) containing all information holographically — every point contains the information of the whole.

2. **The brain does not generate consciousness.** The brain generates a Neuronal Field that extends beyond the body. When this field interacts with the Quantum Field (Lattice), perception — "reality" — emerges as an interference pattern.

3. **This interaction can be consciously modified.** Through meditation, intention, and high syntergy states, one can alter the quality and content of the perception-reality being created. This is how shamans like Doña Pachita could perform psychic surgery, materialize objects, and heal at a distance.

- His laboratory was found completely empty — all equipment, notes, data, experiments gone
- His wife, Tere Mendoza, also disappeared that same day
- No travel records, no struggle, no body, no explanation

Three scenarios:
1. **Silenced by the system** — Someone (CIA/Castaneda group/other) did not want this knowledge public
2. **Exited through the Lattice** — Having mastered the code, he consciously left this reality
